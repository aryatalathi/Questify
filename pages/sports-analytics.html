Expected Goals (xG) in Football
Understanding Expected Goals metric in football analytics:
  • Measures quality of scoring chances based on historical data
  • Considers shot location, angle, and type of play
  • Better predictor of future performance than actual goals
  • Helps identify overperforming/underperforming players
This metric revolutionizes how we evaluate player and team performance.
